Overview

AutoEX brings enterprise-grade automotive diagnostics to MacOS with native support for DrewLinq OBD-II interfaces. Built for mechanics, loggers, truckers, shop owners, and anyone working in the real world with real equipment, AutoEX gives you clarity, insight, and actionable vehicle data instantly.
